Hi! I'm **Deepak G**, an undergraduate student in Aerospace Engineering at IIT Kharagpur.

My research interests lie in  **safe autonomy**, **reinforcement learning**, and **robotics**.

I am currently a **Summer Undergraduate Research Fellow at Caltech**, where I work in the AMBER Lab under the supervision of Prof. Aaron Ames and Dr. Yana Lishkova, developing safe control algorithms for autonomous spacecraft using model predictive control and control barrier functions.

Previously, I worked on reinforcement learning for fixed-wing UAV control at IIIT Hyderabad and nonlinear flight control of airships at CSIR-NAL.
