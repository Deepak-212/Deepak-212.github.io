Schlieren imaging to capture shock wave geometry over Concorde and Su-57 nose models across a range of angles of attack (0°–10°) at 
Mach 2.2. 

**Key Contributions**
- Set up schlieren imaging rig to visualize compressible flow features
- Captured and compared shock structures around Concorde vs Su-57 nose geometries
- Compared experimental shock angles with predictions from Oblique shock relations (θ − β −M charts) for 2D wedge baseline 
Taylor-Maccoll conical flow theory for 3D axisymmetric Concorde nose
